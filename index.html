About 

I am currently working at Myntra, a prominent fashion e-commerce platform in India, where I work as a Senior Product Designer. My primary responsibility is to come up with ideas and execute them in a way that meets both the customer's needs and aligns with design and technology requirements. I work closely with the tech team to oversee the design process from the initial concept to the final product launch. Utilizing user surveys and feedback analysis, I use engaging storytelling methods to craft solutions that precisely cater to their requirements.
