About 

Currently working on a mobile platform at Uvi Health to simplify healthcare regime for women living with chronic health disorders. My role as a UI/UX Designer involves ideation and implementation of solutions overlapping customer needs, design and technology. I collaborate with the tech team driving the design process from conceptualisation to implementation of the final product. With insights from user surveys, I use effective storytelling techniques to address user needs.

I have 5 years of design experience, with 2 years of hands on experience using Figma. Before joining Uvi Health I have worked on two capstones and one design sprint with a 1:1 mentorship from UI/UX Bootcamp by Springboard.
