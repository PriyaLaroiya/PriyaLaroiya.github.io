ABOUT

Let me share something with you that isn't on my résumé.

Who are you?

I am an aspiring UX Designer. Currently living in Delhi, India. I’ve started my career as an Interior/ Visual designer that would lead you to believe that I am passionate about pillows and wall paint. It doesn't, but it gets me thrilled about the prospect of creating a one-of-a-kind experience for space's users.  

Being an Empath, my goal is to employ many tools and approaches used by interior and UX designers to reach the same goal: to create a truly personalized and curated experience for a future user of the product. 

Why are you a designer? 

The turning point was in IKEA where I was working as a Communication and Interior Design Co-worker. I collaborated with the Lead communication designer on upcoming customer-facing projects. I learned that being a UX designer is inspiring since designers can identify people's problems and create solutions for them. I like studying people and the process of thinking solutions. Therefore, I decided to turn my career path to UX design. 

What is your design philosophy? 

*Empathy and focus - empathy with the users and place their needs first.

*Simplicity - less is more, I enjoy pushing myself to understand what is really important and designing simple solutions.

*See feedback as a gift - Feedback helps me to achieve better design and to improve as a designer. 

What do you like to do outside of work? 

I like reading, with various topics including space and humanity books mostly by author Michio Haku, behavioral psychology, entrepreneurship, and productivity.
