Why transition from Interior Designer to UX/UI Designer? 

The turning point was in IKEA where I was working as a Communication and Interior Design Co-worker. I collaborated with the Lead communication designer on upcoming customer-facing offline and online projects and learned that there were unexpected similarities between UX and Interior, for example User Research, their needs and activities, and Customer flow in the layout and functionality of the products which made the field seem even more appealing.

Also, being an empath, my primary goal is to employ many tools and approaches used by UX and interior to reach the same goal: to create a truly personalized and curated experience for a future user of the product.

Therefore, I decided to turn my career path to UX/UI design.
